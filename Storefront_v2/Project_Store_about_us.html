The man, the myth, the legend: George Go

He was the most interesting man in the world. Born and raised in the little town of Crawfordsville, IN, George went on to become a very successful entrepeneur. He was able to land the sister company of Dick's Sporting Goods known as Armadillo Sports. With their partnership, both companies seek out to provide the best and fair priced sports equipment in order to get athletes what they need to be the best they can be on gameday.
